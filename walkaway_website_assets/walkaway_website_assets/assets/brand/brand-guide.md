# WalkAway / Siuil Leat - Website Asset Guide

## Brand positioning
- Name: WalkAway / Siuil Leat
- Tagline: Real Strength is knowing when to walk away
- Mission: Violence prevention and awareness initiative working to reduce unprovoked violence in Ireland through education, awareness campaigns, advocacy, research and community engagement.
- Tone: serious, human, community-led, prevention-focused, respectful and action-orientated.

## Suggested colour palette
These colours are taken from the brochure design and should be used consistently across the website.

- Primary navy: #1E4264
- Action orange: #D83B00
- Soft cream: #F3F2E9
- White: #FFFFFF
- Muted gold line/accent: #A99663
- Body text on navy: #EAF0F5

## Typography direction
Use a clean, accessible sans-serif font. Recommended options:
- Headings: Montserrat, Poppins or Inter SemiBold
- Body: Inter, Open Sans or Arial

## Design notes
- Use navy as the main background for hero/section blocks.
- Use orange for section headings, buttons and calls to action.
- Keep layout simple, strong and trust-building.
- Use white/cream cards for readability.
- Avoid overly decorative design. The message is serious and should feel credible.
